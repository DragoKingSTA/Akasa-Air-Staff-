FLIGHT STAGES

Roles & Responsibilities

Frontline Ambassadors

Passenger Handling & Check-In Services

Managing the airport lobby and check-in counters as the first point of physical contact with passengers.

  • Verifying ticket reservations, travel documents, visas, and identification codes.
  • Assigning seating, managing upgrades, and issuing boarding passes accurately.
  • Inspecting and checking in baggage according to weight limits and security guidelines.
  • Managing special-needs passengers, including wheelchair assistance and unaccompanied minors.

Flight Gateway

Gate Operations & Boarding Control

Coordinating boarding procedures at departure gates to ensure flights depart safely and on schedule.

  • Conducting clear boarding calls, prioritizing families, elderly, and premium flyers.
  • Scanning boarding passes, matching identities, and reconciling passenger manifests.
  • Coordinating with the flight crew, load master, and ramp agents to secure on-time departure.
  • Handling standby lists and seat upgrades dynamically during final boarding phases.

Baggage Integrity

Baggage Services & Recovery

Managing arrival luggage processing and assisting passengers when bags are delayed, damaged, or lost.

  • Logging missing baggage files in systems (WorldTracer) and generating Property Irregularity Reports (PIR).
  • Coordinating with operations teams to locate bags and arrange courier delivery to passengers.
  • Assessing damaged baggage claims and processing reimbursement or repair requests.
  • Managing leftover baggage at arrival carousels and handling customs handovers.

Disruption Shield

Flight Disruption & Conflict Handling

Providing professional, empathetic support during irregular operations (delays, weather cancellations, reroutes).

  • Serving as the calming presence for anxious or frustrated passengers during long delays.
  • Re-routing passenger tickets on alternate flights and processing compensation/refund vouchers.
  • Arranging welfare facilities, meals, water, and hotel accommodations for disrupted flyers.
  • Delivering clear, transparent briefings regarding technical delays or operational situations.

About the Airline

Launched in August 2022, Akasa Air is India's fastest-growing and most dependable airline. Headquartered in Mumbai, the airline is built on a strong foundation of safety, efficiency, customer warmth, and environmental sustainability.

Akasa Air operates a modern, fuel-efficient fleet of Boeing 737 MAX aircraft powered by CFM LEAP-1B engines, contributing to reduced carbon emissions. The airline connects over 20+ domestic and international destinations, offering passengers amenities like spacious seating, USB chargers at every seat, and a customized menu (Café Akasa). Akasa's eco-friendly uniforms for crew and ground staff are made from recycled ocean plastic.
